About Us

Who we are

OUR VISION

At S-Bin, our team of dedicated engineers is the driving force behind our innovative waste sorting and QR code reward system. Our engineers come from diverse backgrounds in environmental science, mechanical engineering, and computer science, bringing a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the table. They are passionate about sustainability and committed to creating cutting-edge technology that simplifies recycling and promotes environmental responsibility. Through rigorous research and development, our engineers have designed the S-Bin to be both user-friendly and highly efficient, ensuring that it meets the highest standards of performance and reliability. Their unwavering dedication to excellence and innovation is what sets S-Bin apart as a leader in waste management solutions. Join us in applauding the brilliant minds behind S-Bin, who are making a significant impact on our journey towards a cleaner, greener future.

OUR PRODUCT

Introducing our state-of-the-art electronic waste bin, the S-Bin E-Core, revolutionizing waste management with its cutting-edge features. The S-Bin E-Core is more than just a receptacle; it's a sophisticated system designed to streamline recycling efforts while incentivizing environmentally conscious behavior. Equipped with advanced sensors and sorting technology, the S-Bin E-Core automatically categorizes recyclable materials upon disposal, ensuring efficient waste segregation. What sets the S-Bin E-Core apart is its seamless integration of QR code scanning functionality. Users can simply scan the QR code on the bin using their smartphones to earn reward points for their eco-friendly actions. These points can then be redeemed for a variety of rewards, motivating individuals to actively participate in recycling initiatives. With the S-Bin E-Core, sustainability meets convenience, making it the ultimate solution for modern waste management needs.

  1. The Idea

    January 16th, 2024

    This phase began with our recognition of the significant problem of waste management and the adverse effects of unmanaged waste on the environment. From initial rough ideas, we began researching and developing the concept of a smart trash bin.

  2. Development starts

    February 28th, 2024

    Subsequently, through research and discussion, we identified the necessary features for a smart trash bin, including automatic waste sorting and notification when full. We spent time analyzing modern technologies such as sensors, machine learning, and IoT to apply them to creating an efficient operational system.

  3. Under Devloperment

    March 20th, 2024

    After outlining the basic concept, we started programming and testing the necessary software and hardware for the smart trash bin. Through iterative testing and refinement, we fine-tuned and perfected the product to ensure the highest stability and performance.

  4. Website launches

    May 20th, 2024

    Simultaneously, we developed a user-facing website accessible to the public. This platform provided users with information about the smart trash bins, their locations, and guidelines for proper waste disposal. Users could also access real-time data on bin availability and capacity, enabling them to make informed decisions when disposing of their waste.

  5. The future of S-Bin

    July 9th, 2024

    Finally, with the completed product, we integrated the smart trash bin into the scientific and technical project, aiming to provide practical benefits and solve the waste management problem effectively. Simultaneously, we also set the goal of developing and deploying widespread applications of this product, from urban areas to public spaces and industrial zones, to create a cleaner and more sustainable living environment.
