S-Bin Bug Bounty Program

Introduction

At S-Bin, we prioritize the security and reliability of our electronic waste bin system. We invite security researchers and ethical hackers to help us identify and fix vulnerabilities through our Bug Bounty Program. Your contributions are essential in making S-Bin a safer and more secure product.

Scope

The Bug Bounty Program covers the following areas:

  • S-Bin hardware and software components
  • QR code scanning feature
  • User account system and data storage
  • S-Bin web and mobile applications

Eligibility

To be eligible for the Bug Bounty Program, you must:

  • Adhere to the rules and guidelines outlined in this policy
  • Be the first to report a specific vulnerability
  • Not publicly disclose the vulnerability before it is fixed
  • Not be an employee of S-Bin or its affiliates

Submission Guidelines

When submitting a vulnerability report, please include the following details:

  • Description: A detailed description of the vulnerability and its potential impact
  • Reproduction Steps: Step-by-step instructions to reproduce the vulnerability
  • Technical Details: Any relevant technical details, including screenshots, logs, or proof-of-concept code
  • Your Contact Information: Your name and email address

Reward

We offer rewards based on the severity and impact of the reported vulnerability. The rewards are categorized as follows:

  • Critical: Up to $100
  • High: Up to $80
  • Medium: Up to $50
  • Low: Up to $10

Disclosure Policy

We are committed to working with the security community to verify and address reported vulnerabilities promptly. You must not disclose any vulnerability details to the public or third parties until we have resolved the issue and given you permission to do so.

Legal

By participating in the S-Bin Bug Bounty Program, you agree to comply with all applicable laws and regulations. Unauthorized access to data or systems, and disruption of S-Bin services, are strictly prohibited and may result in disqualification from the program and legal action.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact us at security@s-bin.com.
