Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Create Ticket

Our website helps users earn reward codes by using coins. Users can earn coins by going to an S-Bin trash can, disposing of trash, and scanning a code that appears afterwards.

To earn coins, users need to go to an S-Bin trash can, dispose of trash, and scan the code that appears after disposal. Each successful scan rewards a certain amount of coins.

Coins you earn can be used to redeem reward codes on our website. These codes can be for discounts, gifts, or other exciting rewards.

You just need to register an account on our website. Then you can go to the nearest S-Bin trash cans to start earning coins.

The number of coins you earn per scan may vary depending on the type of trash and our current policies. Details will be updated on the website.

You can scan codes from S-Bin trash cans supported by our system. Please check the list of supported locations on our website.

S-Bin trash cans will have a specific label and instructions on how to scan the code after disposing of trash. You can find detailed information and illustrations on our website.

If you encounter an issue while scanning the code, please check your internet connection and ensure you followed the instructions correctly. If the problem persists, please contact our customer support for assistance.

There may be a limit on the number of scans per day to ensure fairness and sustainability of the system. Please check our website for more details.

Once you've accumulated enough coins, you can go to the "Redeem Rewards" section on our website, select your desired reward, and follow the steps to redeem it.
